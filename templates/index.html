üå≤ What is Deforestation?

Deforestation refers to large-scale forest clearance, mainly due to human activities. It leads to habitat loss, climate change, and biodiversity threats.

üî• Causes of Deforestation

  • Agriculture: Expanding farmland for crops and livestock.
  • Urbanization: Clearing forests for infrastructure.
  • Illegal Logging: Unsustainable timber harvesting.
  • Mining: Land clearing for mineral extraction.
  • Wildfires: Both natural and human-induced fires.

üåé Consequences

  • Livelihood Impact: Indigenous displacement.
